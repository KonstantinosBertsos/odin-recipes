Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

ingredients

  • Lasagna
  • Pasta sauce
  • Beef & Sausage
  • Mozzarela
  • Riccota Cheese
  • 1 Egg
  • Italian spice mix
  1. Boil the pasta for 5 minutes
  2. In a pan with a little bit of olive oil lightly cook the Beef & Sausage
  3. Throw in the pasta sauce and the spice mix up to taste
  4. Mix the Egg with the Riccota cheese
  5. Time to start layiering up! start with a layer of lasagna followed by the meat sauce riccota mix and mozzarela
  6. Make as many layers as you have ingriends for
  7. In a preheated over of 225 celcius, cook the lasgna for 30 minutes
  8. After you take it out let it rest for 10 minutes before serving
